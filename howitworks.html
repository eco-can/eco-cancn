Step 1: Download our Eco-Can mobile app.

*Available on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

Step 2: Sign up for an account.

Make sure to remember your account details!

Step 3: Start Earning Points

When near a Eco-Can or *Eco-Can Certified Vending machine, scan the QR code or enter the 6 digit code on the machine into the mobile app. Then put in your bottle(s) or can(s). When finished, press the finish button. Your points will go straight into your account.

Step 4: Redeeming Points

Using the points in your account, you can then redeem the points for gift cards or for discounted items with *Eco-Can sponsored companies. To learn more, go here.
